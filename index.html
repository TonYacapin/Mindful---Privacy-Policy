Privacy Policy for Mindful Journal

Last updated: November 2023

Thank you for using Mindful Journal. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Mindful Journal collects and stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Journal Entries: The text content you write in your journal
  • Mood Data: Your selected mood and associated metadata
  • App Preferences: Your selected theme and other app settings
  • Timestamps: Date and time of your journal entries

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to:

  • Provide the core journaling functionality
  • Display your mood statistics and trends
  • Personalize your app experience with themes
  • Maintain your journal history

Data Storage and Security

All your data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. We do not:

  • Transmit your data to any external servers
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Collect any personal identifiers
  • Use your data for advertising or analytics

Third-Party Services

Mindful Journal uses the following third-party services:

  • Expo Haptics: For providing haptic feedback (vibrations) when interacting with the app
  • React Native AsyncStorage: For local data storage on your device

These services only access information necessary to perform their functions and do not collect your journal data.

Your Control Over Your Data

You have complete control over your data:

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • You can delete individual journal entries within the app
  • Uninstalling the app will remove all your data from the device

Children's Privacy

Mindful Journal does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences and does not contain age-restricted content.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
