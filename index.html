Privacy Policy for Mindful Journal

Last updated: November 26, 2025

Thank you for using Mindful Journal. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Mindful Journal collects and stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Journal Entries: The text content you write in your journal
  • Mood Data: Your selected mood and associated metadata
  • App Preferences: Your selected theme, notification settings, and other app configurations
  • Timestamps: Date and time of your journal entries
  • Backup Data: Optional exported backup files of your journal entries

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to:

  • Provide the core journaling functionality
  • Display your mood statistics and trends
  • Personalize your app experience with themes
  • Maintain your journal history
  • Send you local reminder notifications (if enabled)
  • Create backup exports when requested

Data Storage and Security

All your data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. We do not:

  • Transmit your data to any external servers
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Collect any personal identifiers
  • Use your data for advertising or analytics
  • Access your data remotely
  • Track your usage patterns

Notifications

Mindful Journal may send you local notifications as reminders to journal:

  • All notifications are generated locally on your device
  • No notification data is sent to external servers
  • You can enable or disable notifications at any time in the app settings
  • Notification permissions can be managed in your device settings

Third-Party Services

Mindful Journal uses the following third-party services:

  • Expo Haptics: For providing haptic feedback (vibrations) when interacting with the app
  • Expo Notifications: For scheduling local reminder notifications
  • React Native AsyncStorage: For local data storage on your device
  • React Native Calendar Events: For calendar integration features

These services only access information necessary to perform their functions and do not collect or transmit your journal data to external servers.

Your Control Over Your Data

You have complete control over your data:

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • You can edit or delete individual journal entries within the app
  • You can export your data as a backup file
  • You can import previously exported backup data
  • Uninstalling the app will remove all your data from the device

Data Backup and Export

The app provides optional backup functionality:

  • You can export your journal entries to a local file
  • Exported files are stored on your device or in your chosen location
  • We do not automatically upload or sync your backups to any cloud service
  • You are responsible for managing and securing your backup files

Children's Privacy

Mindful Journal does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences and does not contain age-restricted content.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Your Consent

By using Mindful Journal, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the app.
